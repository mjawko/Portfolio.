"Animal sanctuary", what is it?

An animal sanctuary is a place which provides a safe place to animals who were rescued for the rest of their lives.

You may have watched or heard about animals who escaped form a track before the car arrived at a slaughter house.

Some of these animals who succeeded to escape are sent to animal sanctuaries and live without being exploited.

However, it's not everybody to be rescued. Sadly, it happens to many of them to be found by human and be sent back to a slaughter house to be killed.

Vegans criticize meat, fish, egg, dairy, honey, leather, fur, zoos, aquariums, animal testing, hunting...

Because these products and entertainments consist of animal exploitation.
